About

I am a software developer. I enjoy working with open source technologies. I have been working with K-8 student assessment systems for several years now.

I work mostly with PHP, JavaScript, and MySQL. I also write applications using Python, and Golang.
I am a systems administrator. I manage a profitable SAAS product with thousands of active daily users. I have managed production application environments ( physical, virtual, and cloud) for multiple large universities.

I am currently using Amazon Web Services (ELB, EC2, RDS, S3, Elasticache) for most production work.

I have migrated several physical production servers and services to "the cloud" ( Rackspace, and Amazon Web Services ).
